🧪 LabOS Reagent Capture

Capture reagents from any biomedical vendor and route them to LabOS for centralized purchasing.

Connection

Point the extension at your LabOS backend. Captured items will sync automatically.

Pre-supported vendors

These vendors work out of the box — just visit a product page and click the orange "Add to LabOS" button.

Sigma-Aldrich MilliporeSigma Thermo Fisher Invitrogen Life Technologies Fisher Scientific VWR Bio-Rad Abcam NEB IDT R&D Systems Cell Signaling Qiagen Santa Cruz Miltenyi Biotec Eppendorf Beckman Promega Takara Bio

Crowdsourced catalog enrichment (opt-in)

Share parsed product details (anonymized — no prices, no user info) with the LabOS community catalog. Helps the extension work on new vendor sites for everyone. Off by default.

Custom vendors

Add any other vendor you order from. Provide the hostname and (optionally) CSS selectors that identify product name, catalog #, price on their pages. Most sites with schema.org Product microdata will work without any selectors.
